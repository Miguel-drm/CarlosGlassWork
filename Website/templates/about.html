A Journey of Excellence

Founded in 1995, Carlos Glass Work emerged from a vision to revolutionize glass craftsmanship in Cebu.

What began as a modest glass repair workshop has evolved into the region's premier destination for sophisticated glass solutions. Our journey reflects an unwavering dedication to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Today, we proudly stand at the intersection of traditional artistry and cutting-edge technology, delivering unparalleled glass products and services that transform spaces and exceed expectations.
